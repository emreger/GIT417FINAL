Click on the buttons below to see some of my work.

I took this picture right after sunset. It was taken at Imbrie Hall Mcmennamins. I was under a lighted archway with a view on their open green yard. I love the contrast of the lights to the dimming blue sky and the white of the cherry blossom petals.

I live in Portland, OR, and when it snows it is an event. Everyone comes out to play and I went out to get some pretty pictures. I got this one outside of a music studio, the lights reflecting on the snow was stunning! It seems like the snow itself is glowing!

I went to Nice, France during the fall, so it rained on and off. I did not enjoy walking in the rain too much, but after it rained there was so much potential for reflections! As seen in this picture, you can see the yellows and oranges from the building reflecting on the street. I was also lucky to capture this when no one was around.
